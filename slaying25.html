Welcome to Netpedia

Netpedia is an encyclopedia that anyone can edit, and tens of millions already have!
Netpedia's purpose is to benefit readers by acting as a widely accessible and free encyclopedia that contains information on all branches of knowledge. Hosted by the Wikimedia Foundation, Netpedia consists of freely editable content, whose articles also have numerous links to guide readers for more information.
